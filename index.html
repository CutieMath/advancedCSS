We design, manufacture and launch advanced rockets and spacecraft

SpaceX is the only private company capable of returning a spacecraft from low-Earth orbit. In 2012 our Dragon spacecraft became the first commercial spacecraft to deliver cargo to and from the International Space Station. In May 2020 we bacame the first private company to take humans to ISS (Crew Dragon Demo-2 Mission). In that mission, both astronuts and Falcon 9 first stage booster returned to earth safely.

Building on the achievements of Falcon 9 and Falcon Heavy, SpaceX is working on a next generation of fully reusable launch vehicles that will be the most powerful ever built, capable of carrying humans to Mars and other destinations in the solar system.
